Redeemed Christian Church of God

The Redeemed Christian Church of God was established in 1952 by Pa Josiah Akindayomi. The church was formerly called Ogo Oluwa Society before God gave the name "Redeemed Christian Church of God" to him by divine revelation.

About the General Overseer

The general overseer of the mission today is Pastor Enoch Adejare Adeboye, a revered and anointed man of God. He took over the leadership of the mission in the year 1982, now 40years ago. Through him, the mision has expanded to over 198 countries of the world. He is blessed with a wife, Pastor Mrs Folu Adeboye and five children.

Vision and Mission statement of the Church

The following are our mission and goal as members of the Redeemed Christian Church of God:

  1. To make heaven.
  2. To take as many people as possible with us.
  3. To have a member of R.C.C.G in every family of all nation.
  4. To accomplish no. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  5. To accomplish no. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutesdriving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
